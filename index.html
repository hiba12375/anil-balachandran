Anil Balachandran The King Maker

Mr.Anil Balachandran was born on 1 st January 1987 (New Year Baby) and brought up in a middle class family in Kayamkulam, Alleppy district, Kerala. He had his schooling also in Alleppy and joined Kerala University in 2004 – 2007 to complete his graduation in BSc Mathematics. Later he shifted to Bangalore for higher studies and joined Acharya Institute of Management (VTU-Visweswarayya Technological University) where he had mastered his MBA in Sales & Marketing during 2007 -2009. Recently he received doctorate in Global Business Management from Al-Farabi Khazak National University.

What Can a Good Business Coach Do for You?

Clarity & Direction

A business coach helps you set clear goals and build a step-by-step strategy to reach them, so you always know what to focus on next.

Accountability for Growth

They keep you accountable, ensuring you stay on track and follow through with your plans, just like a personal trainer, but for your business.

Smarter Decision-Making

With expert guidance and an outside perspective, a coach helps you avoid costly mistakes and make confident, informed decisions.

Improved Profitability & Systems

They help you streamline operations, manage time better, and identify hidden revenue opportunities, leading to higher profits and less stress.
